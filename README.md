# 📊 Marketing Intelligence Report

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Marketing Intelligence Report** is a Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Power BI** to analyze campaign performance, advertising engagement, and audience behavior.

The objective of this project is to support data-driven marketing decisions by identifying high-performing campaigns, evaluating advertising platforms, and understanding audience engagement.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Marketing teams invest significant budgets across multiple campaigns and digital platforms.

This dashboard answers key business questions such as:

- Which campaigns receive the highest investment?
- Which advertising platforms generate the most engagement?
- Which audience groups respond most effectively?
- Where should future marketing budgets be allocated?

The report converts raw marketing data into actionable business insights for strategic decision-making.

# 🔍 Key Insights

- Google Ads generated the highest audience engagement based on total ad clicks.
- UG Admission campaigns accounted for the largest marketing investment.
- The 17–21 age group produced the highest application volume.
- Campaign performance differed across platforms, indicating opportunities to optimize marketing spend.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in Google Ads due to stronger audience engagement.
- Review campaigns with high costs but lower application performance.
- Prioritize campaigns targeting the 17–21 age segment.
- Monitor Cost Per Click (CPC) and Conversion Rate before increasing marketing budgets.
- Continuously evaluate campaign effectiveness using business KPIs.
